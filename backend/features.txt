*FEATURES RELEVANT TO SHIFT SCHEDULING*

    staff
        - hire_date : later hire -> less exp -> more stressed
        - status: Available or Not available -> directly affects if theyre allowed to have shifts
        - primary_department: Give preference to shifts here
    
    department:
        - department_name: ED > ICU > General
    
    incident_exposure:
        - severity_level: HIGH > MEDIUM > LOW
        - emotional_weight_score: from 0-10, higher score -> more stress -> give less shifts
        - recorded_at: recent incidents are weighted more towards shift preference

    workload_record:
        - week_start_date: recent records have higher weights towards shift preference
        - total_hours: more weekly hours -> more stress -> give less shifts
        - night_shifts: higher num of night shifts -> more stress -> give less night shifts in future
        - consecutive_days: higher num -> more stress -> give more breaks
        - overtime_hours: higher num -> more stress -> give less shifts
    
    wellness_score:
        this should be updated each week for each staff member based on current week events and previous scores
        - fatigue_score
        - stress_score
        - burnout_risk
